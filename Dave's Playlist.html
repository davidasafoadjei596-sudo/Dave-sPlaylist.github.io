🎶 About My Music Love

Music is the shortcut to every emotion I've ever felt. The haunting harmonies of Billie Eilish, the raw, heartfelt lyrics of James Arthur, the poetic storytelling of Santan Dave, the infectious Afrobeats energy of Asake, and the powerful vulnerability of Zoe Wees — each artist adds a unique color to my life. Whether I'm coding, driving through Accra, or just reflecting, a great song transforms the ordinary into magic. That's why I built this playlist: to share the soundtrack of my soul.
